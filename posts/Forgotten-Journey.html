Forgotten Journey

High Concept: Forgotten Journey is a puzzle-oriented platformer, where the player will follow a linear path jumping, climbing, and solving puzzles with the help of an AI companion that will follow her.

Instructor: Daniel Andia

Engine: Unreal Engine 4

Language: C++/Unreal Blueprints

Release: Steam

Overview: Forgotten Journey is a project developed by Asvaq Games, a team of 4th Year students at DigiPen Institute of Technology Europe - Bilbao in a 9-month development cycle using Unreal Engine 4.

Role: Gameplay Programmer

Developed the main character of the game, motion kinematics, animations and climbing system mechanics.

Implemented the AI companion animations.

Worked with UE4 as a professional game engine for this project.

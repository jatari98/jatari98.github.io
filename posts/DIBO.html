DIBO

High Concept: DIBO is a 2D stealth platformer game in which a clumsy chameleon will prove that defense is the best offense!

Instructor: Thomas Komair

Engine: 2D Game Engine made from Scratch

Language: C++

Release: DigiPen Gallery

Overview: DIBO is the second project I worked on at Digipen during the my Sophomore year. It took 9 months of development using a component based engine made from scratch in C++. The team was composed of 4 programmers and 6 artists.

Role: Gameplay Programmer

Participated in the creation of an C++ component based 2D game engine from scratch by developing the state machine system and some of the character behaviours and animations.

Programmed an interactive object system used for in-game puzzles and combinations.
